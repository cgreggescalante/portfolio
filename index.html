About Me

I am an avid programmer, working primarily in Python, Java, C#, and Typescript, though I have some familiarity working with C++, JavaScript, and R. I have a great enjoyment of learning, and I am constantly searching for new technologies, techniques, and methodologies that I can implement in my projects. For myself, none of my projects are ever really complete. As I learn, I often return to older projects to improve their performance, usability, or to expand upon then in ways that were previously beyond my abilities.

I am passionate about gleaming new insights from the real world through code, and using using that new knowledge to better the world. An ideal position for me would be one in which I am able to make visible impacts on the world around me.

This website is built with Next.js and Typescript, and hosted through GitHub Pages. I am currently working on adding to this site to highlight my most interesting projects.

Outside of software development, I love spending time in the outdoors, running, biking, skiing, and just about anything else that gets me moving. I also enjoy solving a variety of different puzzles. Recently that includes variant Sudoku, crosswords, and ProjectEuler problems. For the latter, I have completed 164 problems to date, placing me in the top 0.5% worldwide.
